\caption{\textbf{Architecture ceiling across the four-cell $2\times2$ factorial ---
subject-level 5-fold CV balanced accuracy by architecture class and dataset.}
The ceiling in each column converges within a $\approx$\!0.15 BA band regardless of architecture class (classical ML through 1.4\,B-parameter FMs), supporting the claim that the per-cell BA ceiling is a \emph{task property} rather than a model-capacity artefact (Appendix\,C).
All values are 3-seed mean $\pm$ sample standard deviation (seeds 42/123/2024, ddof\,=\,1) under subject-disjoint 5-fold CV.
\textbf{EEGMAT}: within-subject $\times$ separable (72 rec / 36 subj);
\textbf{SleepDep}: within-subject $\times$ null-indistinguishable (72 / 36);
\textbf{ADFTD}: between-subject $\times$ separable (65 / 65, split1 binary);
\textbf{Stress}: between-subject $\times$ null-indistinguishable (70 / 17).
\emph{Classical ML}: PSD + power spectral features (101-dim), \texttt{class\_weight=balanced}; source: \texttt{exp02\_classical\_dass/\{dataset\}/}.
\emph{Deep CNN}: trained from scratch; EEGNet lr\,=\,1e-3 (ADFTD/EEGMAT/SleepDep), 5e-4 (Stress); ShallowConvNet lr\,=\,5e-4 (ADFTD/EEGMAT/SleepDep), 1e-4 (Stress); source: \texttt{exp15\_nonfm\_baselines/}.
\emph{FM LP}: per-window frozen features, L2-regularised logistic regression, recording-level majority vote (\texttt{class\_weight=balanced}); source: \texttt{perwindow\_lp\_all/}.
\emph{FM FT}: full fine-tuning with per-cell recipe (lr\,=\,1e-5, enc\_scale\,=\,0.1, wd\,=\,0.05 for EEGMAT/ADFTD/Stress; wd\,=\,0.01 for SleepDep); source: \texttt{exp04/exp07/exp\_newdata/hp\_sweep}.
Chance level = 0.50.}
